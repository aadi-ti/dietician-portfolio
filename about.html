Our Philosophy

At the core of Dt. Joshi’s practice lies the belief that every individual is biochemically unique. Her method is rooted in listening deeply — understanding lifestyle, stress levels, sleep patterns, and goals — before designing a plan that is scientifically sound, flexible, and sustainable.

She doesn't believe in temporary diets. She works alongside clients to shift perspectives, develop mindful habits, and turn food into a tool for healing, strength, and joy.
